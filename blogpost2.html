Forget-Me-Not

September 29, 2024 by Hania

Myosotis (/ˌmaɪəˈsoʊtɪs/ MY-ə-SOH-tiss) is a genus of flowering plants in the family Boraginaceae. The name comes from the Ancient Greek μυοσωτίς "mouse's ear", which the foliage is thought to resemble. In the Northern Hemisphere, they are colloquially known as forget-me-nots.


In the history of art, the forget-me-not is used to remember loved ones who have died, and so is very common in funerary portraits.
